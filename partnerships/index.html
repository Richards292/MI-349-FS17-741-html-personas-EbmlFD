Description

Many companies share our goal of creating an affordable environment, and would like to bring their staff on board. We should be more than willing to offer partnerships for extended interacting between these companies and Star Treaders

Quote from a partnership candidate

"Star Treaders has a really great thing going here! We simply don't have the resources to do what they're doing right now, but are very excited to work together with them for the betterment of us all."

Who is it?

We will be offering many partnerships with companies who want to engage their employees in new environments. These will be individuals somewhere between students and researchers in needs and commitment.

Goals and attitudes

The primary motivation for these groups would likely be less about research and education, and more about team building in a unique environment. We would host company retreats on shorter trips than the students and researchers would be on.

Behavior

Behavior of this group could be harder to predict, as they would be known only by their employer. Regardless, we would need to provide excersize and entertainment spaces.
